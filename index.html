F.A.Q

Is the product applicable to every types of shoes?

As for now, ONECL offers the best experience in low top sneakers. But we are planning to expand our product to fit into various types including high top as well.

My guides fall out every time I tighten my shoes. What's wrong?

Please make sure both of the guides' legs are fully inserted into your shoe's eyelets.

Why are the laces falling out of the guides?

You must pull in the lace into the guides until you hear a click or can see that the lace is inside the hook of the guide.

Is it ok to wash my shoes?

Yes. Because of our sticky resistant tape, any shoes with ONECL is perfectly fine to be washed in the laundry or the cleaners.

Do you ship overseas?

Yes. We make sure that your product will arrive intact anywhere in the world.
